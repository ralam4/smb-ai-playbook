# Write Listings That Sell and Leads That Convert
### AI Playbook for Small Business — Real Estate Agent Guide

Generate standout listing descriptions, build a lead nurture sequence, and create a listing presentation that wins clients.

---

**Instructions:** Open ChatGPT or Claude. Copy and paste each prompt in order. Fill in the bracketed sections with your own information.

---

## Step 1: Gather Your Listing Details

For a current or upcoming listing, collect: full property details (beds, baths, sqft, lot size, year built, recent upgrades), neighborhood highlights (schools, dining, commute times, parks), unique selling points (views, layout, history, features), target buyer profile (first-time buyers, families, investors, downsizers), and 3-5 comparable recent sales with prices.

---

## Prompt 1: Generate Standout Listing Descriptions

```
I'm a real estate agent and I need compelling listing descriptions for a property.

Property details:
- Address: [ADDRESS or general area]
- Type: [single-family, condo, townhouse, etc.]
- Beds/Baths: [NUMBER]
- Square footage: [NUMBER]
- Lot size: [SIZE]
- Year built: [YEAR]
- Recent upgrades: [LIST — e.g. 'new kitchen 2024', 'updated HVAC', 'fresh landscaping']
- Unique features: [LIST — e.g. 'corner lot', 'mountain views', 'original hardwood floors', 'ADU potential']
- Neighborhood highlights: [LIST — e.g. 'walkable to downtown', 'top-rated school district', '10 min to freeway']
- Target buyer: [e.g. 'young families', 'remote workers wanting space', 'investors']
- List price: $[PRICE]

Please create:
1. A full MLS description (250-300 words) — compelling, specific, avoids clichés like 'charming' or 'won't last long'
2. A luxury/elevated version of the same listing (if applicable)
3. Three social media captions — one for Instagram (with hashtag suggestions), one for Facebook, one for LinkedIn
4. An email announcement I can send to my database with subject line
5. A one-line 'hook' I can use in ads or flyers
```

## Prompt 2: Build a Lead Nurture Sequence

```
I want to create a follow-up sequence for new buyer and seller leads so no one falls through the cracks.

My details:
- My name: [NAME]
- My brokerage: [BROKERAGE]
- My target area: [CITY/NEIGHBORHOOD]
- Where my leads come from: [e.g. 'Zillow, open houses, referrals, social media, website']
- My typical response time currently: [e.g. 'same day' or 'whenever I remember']

Please create two sequences:

BUYER LEAD SEQUENCE (5 touches):
1. Initial response (send within 5 minutes of inquiry) — acknowledge, ask one qualifying question
2. Value-add follow-up (Day 2) — share a relevant listing or market insight, no pressure
3. Market insight (Week 1) — brief market update for their target area, position yourself as the expert
4. Soft check-in (Week 3) — 'still looking?' with a helpful resource attached
5. Re-engagement (Month 2) — if no response, a final friendly touchpoint with an easy reply option

SELLER LEAD SEQUENCE (5 touches):
1. Initial response — acknowledge interest, offer a free home value estimate
2. Value-add (Day 3) — share a recent comparable sale in their area with brief analysis
3. Market positioning (Week 1) — 'here's what's happening in your neighborhood' with data
4. Soft ask (Week 3) — offer a no-obligation listing consultation
5. Re-engagement (Month 2) — seasonal or market-triggered check-in

For each message: provide text/SMS version (under 160 chars) and email version.
```

---

## Prompt 3: Create a Seller Listing Presentation Outline

```
I need a listing presentation framework I can customize for each potential seller.

My details:
- My name and brokerage: [NAME, BROKERAGE]
- My experience: [YEARS in business, transactions closed, areas of specialty]
- My marketing approach: [e.g. 'professional photography, social media, open houses, broker tours, targeted digital ads']
- What makes me different: [LIST 2-3 differentiators]

Please create:
1. A listing presentation outline (8-10 slides/sections) covering: why me, my marketing plan, pricing strategy, timeline and process, and what sellers can expect
2. A pricing conversation script — how to handle 'I want to list higher than comps suggest' professionally
3. Three 'closing' questions I can ask at the end of the presentation to move toward signing
4. A follow-up email template to send after the listing appointment if they didn't sign on the spot
5. A one-page 'leave behind' summary I can print — highlights of my marketing plan and recent results
```

---

## What to Expect

**If it works well:** Listing descriptions that took 30 minutes to write can be drafted in 2 minutes. The lead nurture sequence ensures no lead goes more than 48 hours without a touchpoint.

**If it needs tweaking:** AI-generated listing copy needs your local knowledge. Always add neighborhood-specific details the AI can't know.

**Long-term value:** The lead nurture sequence from Step 3 is the highest-ROI output — consistent follow-up is the #1 differentiator between agents who convert and those who don't.
